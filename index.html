We Are Helping Hands Foundation

Our Mission is To inspire breakthroughs in the way the world treats children, and to achieve immediate, and lasting change in their lives

The Helping Hands Foundation is a not-for-profit organization headquartered in SRMIST Chennai, India. The Foundation strives to bring changes to every needy child by implementing different Programmes. Helping Hands aims to counter malnutrition and support the right to education of children hailing from socio-economically challenging backgrounds.
